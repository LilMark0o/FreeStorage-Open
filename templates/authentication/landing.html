About Us

Free Storage: A free open-source project to empower users with limitless file storage and sharing capabilities.

Free Storage is a project designed to provide users with a reliable and unlimited way to upload, store, and share files online. Developed by me, Marco Alejandro Ramírez Camacho, a Systems Engineering and Computer Science student from the University of Los Andes, this platform aims to make file management seamless and efficient for everyone.

  • Open-source project, welcoming contributions from the community.
  • Built with a focus on user experience and data security.
  • Empowering users to share files easily with friends and family.

With a background in programming, databases, and machine learning, I've developed this platform to bridge the gap between user needs and technological solutions. My experience includes optimizing data models and analyzing large datasets, which enhances the performance and reliability of Free Storage.

Join us in transforming the way you store and share your files—safely, freely, and without limits!
